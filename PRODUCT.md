# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Individual investors, traders, and finance professionals who need fast, trustworthy access to global market news and data. They're scanning during market hours — at their desk, on mobile during commute, or on a second monitor. Speed and clarity are paramount.

## Product Purpose

MarketPulse delivers real-time financial market news and data across global stocks, commodities, ETFs, mutual funds, forex, and economic indicators. It aggregates from public RSS feeds into a clean, authoritative reading experience. Success means the user finds the signal in the noise — the one story that matters to their portfolio today — in under 30 seconds.

## Brand Personality

Modern & Sharp. Confident, fast, data-driven, and precise. Authoritative without being stuffy; editorial without being verbose.

## Anti-references

- Yahoo Finance cluttered homepage (too many widgets, low signal-to-noise)
- Generic AI-gradient SaaS landing pages (blue-purple glow, centered hero over mesh)
- Overly casual or playful fintech apps (too much color, too little gravitas)
- Three-equal-feature-card layouts (default AI scaffolding)

## Design Principles

1. **Signal over noise.** Every element earns its place. Reduce cognitive load through generous whitespace, clear hierarchy, and restrained color.
2. **Data-forward, not data-dense.** Present market data with clarity and breathing room. Use typographic hierarchy over chart junk.
3. **Editorial authority.** The visual language borrows from premium financial print (Bloomberg, FT) — confident typography, restrained palette, intentional rhythm.
4. **Fast by feel.** The interface communicates speed through visual compression (tight leading on display type, minimal chrome, high data density when it counts).
5. **Three-canvas rhythm.** Indigo navy hero → white content body → deep teal closing CTA. The page tells a visual story through deliberate surface changes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast minimum (4.5:1 body, 3:1 large text)
- Skip-to-content link for keyboard users
- Semantic heading hierarchy and landmark regions
- prefers-reduced-motion respected for all animations
- Touch targets ≥44px on interactive elements
